Garlic Butter Pasta

Description

A simple and flavorful pasta dish with garlic and butter, perfect for a quick meal.

Indgredients

  • 100g pasta (any kind)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions and drain.
  2. In a pan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and sautÃ© until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  4. Toss the cooked pasta in the garlic butter, and season with salt and pepper.
