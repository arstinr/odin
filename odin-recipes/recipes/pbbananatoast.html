Peanut Butter Banana Toast

Description

A quick and easy breakfast or snack featuring creamy peanut butter and sweet banana on toasted bread.

Indgredients

  • 1 slice of bread
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 banan

Steps

  1. Toast the slice of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter evenly over the toasted bread.
  3. Slice the banana and arrange the slices on top of the peanut butter.
